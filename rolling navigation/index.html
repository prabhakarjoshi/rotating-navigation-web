Welcome! to the CORBETT

Some information

The Jim Corbett National Park is situated at an approximate distance of 260 Kms from the National Capital Delhi. The small town near Corbett is Ramnagar which forms the headquarters of CTR. Ramnagar is covered by forests of Jim Corbett and therefore it is also known as Corbett city. In picture shown tigeress hunting its prey nearly 1 km away from city's barrage.

Some more information

The city is well connected by road or rail with major cities of India like Nainital ,Moradabad, Bareilly and Delhi. The small city Ramnagar has a very good road network that connects the different cities of India. State government buses arrives at a short interval of time from Haldwani , Moradabad, Delhi, that reaches directly Ramnagar. Corbett is only 15 Kms from Ramnagar town. Roadways Station and railway station both are approximately 2 km apart from each other ,since the city is small ,every facility is densed here and so tourists are not going to have any problem .

last information

Ramnagar is the only nearest railway station to Jim Corbett National Park.A direct train to Ramnagar runs from Delhi. Alternatively, one can reach up to Haldwani/ Kashipur/ Kathgodam and come to Ramnagar by road.
